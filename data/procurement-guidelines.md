# Procurement Guidelines

## Vendor Selection
All new vendors must undergo a qualification process including financial assessment, quality audit, and reference checks. The minimum vendor rating for continued business is 3.5 out of 5.0.

## Purchase Order Approval Matrix
- Orders up to 10,000 AED: Purchasing Officer approval
- Orders 10,001 - 50,000 AED: Procurement Manager approval
- Orders 50,001 - 200,000 AED: Finance Director approval
- Orders above 200,000 AED: CEO approval required

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are Net 30. Early payment discounts of 2% Net 10 may be negotiated for orders above 100,000 AED. All payment terms must be documented in the vendor master record in D365.

## Emergency Procurement
Emergency purchases bypassing standard approval require written justification and post-facto approval within 48 hours. Emergency procurement is limited to 25,000 AED per instance.
