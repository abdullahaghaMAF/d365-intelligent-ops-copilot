# Inventory Management Policy

## Reorder Point Policy
All SKUs must maintain a minimum safety stock of 15% above the average monthly demand. When inventory falls below the reorder point, an automatic purchase requisition must be generated within 24 hours.

## Warehouse Transfer Rules
Inter-warehouse transfers require approval from the regional warehouse manager for quantities exceeding 500 units. Transfers under 500 units can be initiated by warehouse supervisors. All transfers must be completed within 48 hours of approval.

## Cycle Counting
Each warehouse must complete a full cycle count quarterly. High-value items (unit cost above 1000 AED) must be counted monthly. Discrepancies above 2% must be investigated and documented within 5 business days.

## Dead Stock Policy
Items with no movement for 180 days are classified as dead stock. Dead stock must be reviewed by the procurement team monthly. Disposal or markdown decisions must be made within 30 days of classification.
