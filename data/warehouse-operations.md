# Warehouse Operations Manual

## Receiving Process
All inbound shipments must be inspected within 4 hours of arrival. Quality inspection is mandatory for all items. Damaged goods must be photographed and reported in D365 within 24 hours. GRN (Goods Receipt Note) must be posted in D365 same day as physical receipt.

## Picking and Packing
Orders must be picked within 2 hours of release. FIFO (First In First Out) method is mandatory for all perishable items. Packing must include item verification scan, weight check, and documentation. Shipping labels must be generated from D365 Commerce.

## Health and Safety
All warehouse staff must wear PPE including safety boots, high-visibility vests, and hard hats in forklift zones. Maximum stacking height is 4 pallets. Fire exits must remain clear at all times. Monthly safety drills are mandatory.

## KPI Targets
- Order fulfillment rate: 98.5% minimum
- Inventory accuracy: 99% minimum
- Receiving dock-to-stock time: Under 4 hours
- Pick accuracy: 99.5% minimum
